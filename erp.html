Student ERP

Student ERP project is a comprehensive web application designed to enhance the overall student experience. It offers a wide range of functionalities and tools to simplify various aspects of academic life, from assessment and resource access to financial management and performance tracking. This platform serves as a one-stop solution for students to efficiently manage their educational journey and achieve academic success.

Key Features:
1. Assessment Tests: Students can take assessment tests and quizzes online, providing a convenient way to evaluate their knowledge and understanding of course materials.
2. Subject Notes and Digital Books: Access to a digital library where students can download and view subject notes and textbooks, making study materials easily accessible.
3. Academic Calendar: Students can view their academic calendar, which includes important dates such as class schedules, exams, holidays, and assignment deadlines.
4. Fee Payment: Secure online fee payment functionality enables students to pay their college fees conveniently through the web app, reducing the need for physical transactions.
5. Online Lectures: Professors can schedule and conduct online lectures, and students can join these sessions directly through the application. It promotes remote learning and ensures students never miss a class.
6. Performance Calculator (ML-based): A unique feature powered by Machine Learning (ML) algorithms that calculates and presents students with an accurate assessment of their academic performance over the year. This tool helps students track their progress effectively.
