MedConnect

This healthcare application is designed to cater to various types of users, including patients, lab representatives, and administrators. It streamlines processes like scheduling doctor consultations and lab appointments, eliminating the need for traditional, time-consuming methods. Patients can access certified physicians remotely through audio and video calls, providing vital healthcare services to those in geographically isolated regions. Security and data protection are paramount in this system to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of patient records, including medication prescriptions and lab test reports. Ultimately, the project's goal is to reduce unnecessary travel to healthcare facilities, making healthcare services more accessible and efficient.

Key Features:
1. Easy and user-friendly mode of login which is authenticated with email based OTP each time the user i.e., patients’ or doctors’ login.
2. It offers secured, decentralized storage of essential and important documents of the user, also offers easy retrieval of those documents including prescriptions.
3. The medical history of the patient is either visible to the operating hospital or in the patient’s profile. This data cannot be used by any other people.
4. The security measures such as privacy, authentication, authorization, integrity are successfully achieved.
5. The system is easy to understand by the layman persons too.
